It describes the process of checking of grammatic the German handwritten text with noted with your **native language**.

You can change the instructions (see below) as you wish.

## Process

Open the ChatGPT.
Attach a photo of the letter to your message.
Paste the prompt below.
Choose the model `o4` or `o3`.
Look and learn, bitte.

## Prompt

```
v1.6.0

I wrote a German letter, I want to correct my mistakes and learn to write competently.

<extraction>
Manually extract the text from the photo.
Leave the original text as well.
</extraction>

<chunks>
Continue processing until all chunks are processed.
</chunks>


<step_1>
Write out the recognized text without correcting errors.
</step_1>


<step_2>
Translate this letter into Ukrainian so that I understand how a native German speaker will perceive my letter.
</step_2>


<step_3>
Look at the photo and form a table:

1/ Original sentence.
2/ The corrected (or not) sentence.
3/ What mistakes or inaccuracies were made. If everything is correct, leave the cell empty. Explanation of the mistakes in Ukrainian. Numerate the winks within the speech. How you can write better and more naturally. Other variants to convey the same idea.

Don't use codeblocks in a table to preserve formatting.
</step_3>

<step_4>
Make a summary of the mistakes and suggest modern efficient ways not to make them again.
Answer in the same language as you explained above.
</step_4>

```
